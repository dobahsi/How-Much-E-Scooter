Data and methods

We examine the lifecycle carbon emissions and consumer ownership costs of a total of 156 scooters in Taiwan—including all the domestic internal combustion engine scooters powered by gasoline and battery electric scooters that are available on the market in 2021.

Details on the calculation of costs, greenhouse gas emissions, and emission targets can be found in the reference [1] below.

Reference

[1]: Yuan-Hsi Chien, I-Yun Lisa Hsieh, Tsung-Heng Chang, Beyond personal vehicles: How electrifying scooters will help achieve climate mitigation goals in Taiwan, Energy Strategy Reviews, Volume 45, 2023 (Open Access).
